Introduction

At BLDEACET Central Library, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to ensure its security.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, student ID, and contact details when you register.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, and borrowed book history.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your library account and provide personalized services.
  • To improve our website, services, and resources based on user feedback.
  • To notify you about updates, events, or changes in library policies.

Data Security

We employ modern security measures such as encryption and secure access protocols to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute safety of your information.

Sharing Your Information

Your personal information is not shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or for system maintenance by trusted service providers.

Your Rights

  • Access your data and request corrections.
  • Withdraw consent for certain uses of your data.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated information.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page, with the last update date displayed below.
